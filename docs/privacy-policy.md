## 1. Introduction

Car Post All ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Car Post All mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect:

- **Email address** -- used for account authentication, password recovery, and essential service communications.
- **Password** -- stored as a salted cryptographic hash. We never store or have access to your plaintext password.

### 2.2 License Plate Numbers

You provide one or more vehicle license plate numbers to claim as your own. Plate numbers are normalized (uppercased, spaces and hyphens removed) and stored in our database. Other users can send messages to a plate number but cannot see who owns it unless you choose to respond.

### 2.3 Messages

Messages you send and receive through the App are stored on our servers. Message content includes text you author and metadata such as timestamps and read status.

### 2.4 Device Information

When you enable push notifications, we collect your Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token. This token is used solely to deliver push notifications to your device. We do not collect device identifiers, hardware model, or operating system version beyond what FCM requires.

### 2.5 Usage Data

We collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., number of messages sent, active sessions) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

### 2.6 Advertising Data

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect device advertising identifiers and usage data according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not share your account information or message content with advertising partners.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively for:

- **Providing the service** -- delivering messages between users based on license plate numbers.
- **Authentication** -- verifying your identity when you log in.
- **Push notifications** -- alerting you to new messages and replies.
- **Moderation** -- reviewing reported content to enforce our Terms of Service.
- **Service improvement** -- analyzing anonymous usage patterns to improve reliability and features.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, with the following exceptions:

- **Legal requirements** -- we may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Service providers** -- we use infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, database hosting) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
- **Advertising** -- Google AdMob operates within the App and may collect data independently per Google's policies. We do not provide AdMob with your account or message data.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on servers located in [REGION/COUNTRY].
- Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
- Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a per-user salt.
- Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- We perform regular backups and security audits.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained as long as your account is active.
- **Messages** are retained as long as either the sender or recipient has an active account.
- **FCM tokens** are removed when you log out or uninstall the App.
- **Deleted accounts** -- upon account deletion, all personal data (email, plates, messages, device tokens) is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate data may be retained.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data by contacting us.
- **Correct** inaccurate data through your account settings or by contacting us.
- **Delete your account** at any time from within the App or by contacting us. This permanently removes all associated personal data.
- **Withdraw consent** for push notifications by disabling them in your device settings.
- **Request data export** -- contact us to receive a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you also have rights under GDPR including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will delete that data promptly.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify active users via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
